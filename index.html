How to Use the 4-7-8 Technique

4s

Inhale through your nose quietly.

7s

Hold your breath comfortably.

8s

Exhale through your mouth with a whoosh sound.

The 4-7-8 breathing technique, developed by Dr. Andrew Weil, is a rhythmic breathing pattern that acts as a natural tranquilizer for the nervous system. Regular practice can help reduce anxiety, manage stress, and improve sleep quality.
